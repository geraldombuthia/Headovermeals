About Us

HeadOverMeals is a web application that provides a variety of recipes to its users. Its main purpose is to diversify meal menus for all its users allowing those passionate about cooking to improve on their skills while also considering those whose cooking skills or passion might be lacking. Its designed to ensure optimum levels of convenience and client satisfaction. The initial design and development of the project was started on the 5th August 2020

About the developer

HeadOverMeals is designed and built by Gerald Mbuthia. He describes himself as an ardent learner of tech with the purpose of finding solutions for his clients.
